Controls:

Character Selection:

  • A/D, ←/→ - Change head
  • S, ↓ - Change cleat
  • Space - Start game

Gameplay:

  • Player 1: Arrow keys (Move: ←/→, Jump: ↑, Kick: ↓)
  • Player 2: WASD (Move: A/D, Jump: W, Kick: S)
